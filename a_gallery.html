My Art Journey

I began my professional journey with the choice of attending a technical college in Innsbruck, specialising in fine arts and historic restoration. This education provided me with many opportunities to showcase my analogue art skills and experiment with different techniques. Over time, however, I found myself increasingly drawn to digital art, particularly inspired by my passion for video games.

These inspirations led me to my current path - studying Game Development at FH Salzburg. Although my studies are primarily based in computer science, I actively pursue various side projects centred on game art. My work includes illustration, concept art, 2D animation and more, all areas I'm deeply passionate about.
